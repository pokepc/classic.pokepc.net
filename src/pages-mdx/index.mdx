Welcome, Trainer! PokéPC Classic (formerly known as SuperEffective.gg) has been your trusted Pokédex Tracker companion
since 2022 on the ultimate quest to catch 'em all! Whether you're exploring on your phone or computer, we've got awesome
tools ready for your adventure: the [National Pokédex](/apps/pokedex), the Livingdex or
[Living Pokédex Tracker](/apps/livingdex) with its handy Box Organization system. The classic site covers every Pokédex
(national and regional) from all games up to Legends Z-A!

> Ready for the next generation? Check out our exciting new successor, [PokéPC](https://pokepc.net/?ref=supereffective)!
> The main PokéPC website is now home to all current and future games, starting after Legends Z-A. The adventure
> continues there!

**What amazing features await you in our Classic Livingdex & Pokédex Tracker?**

- **Every species and form** officially revealed that can transfer to Pokémon HOME from Generations 1 through 9.
- **Track your collection** across all current main series games, including Legends Z-A, Scarlet and Violet, Pokémon
  HOME, and Pokémon GO!
- **Organize your boxes** beautifully with all your Pokémon displayed just the way you like—your path to that perfect
  Livingdex starts here! Do you need more customization? Are you a Ribbon/Mark collector? We've got you covered in the
  [new PokéPC website](https://pokepc.net/?ref=supereffective).
- **Hunt for special variants** like Megas, Gigantamax and Alpha Pokémon.
- **Shiny collector mode** for all those gorgeous sparkling Pokémon.
